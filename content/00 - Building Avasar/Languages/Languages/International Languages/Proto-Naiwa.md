## Overview
An [[International Languages|International Language]] that was spoken long ago. The precursor to the modern day language [[Naiwa]]. Evolved from [[Proto-Belleros-Elisiparian]].

- Noc - Dark

- Ensa - Element
- Zer - Center
- Dar - Day
